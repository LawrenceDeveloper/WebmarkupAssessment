Whoop

The WHOOP is a advanced fitness wearable device that helps you track your fitness. It can monitor a lot of things like your heart rate, blood oxygen levels, sleep cycle, and present it to you in the WHOOP app. With this information you can tell when to exercise and when to rest. This help you a ton in staying fit, while also not exercising too much. This wearable is truly great!
